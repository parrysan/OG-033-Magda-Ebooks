Market assessment

Verdict — RESHAPE

The gap is real. The current plan isn't quite the right shape to fill it.

Nobody found sells independent, seller-side advice — every free "how to sell to a developer" guide is written by the developer or a commission-based intermediary. That confirms the core idea. But three assumptions need adjusting: land sellers are one-time, search-driven buyers rather than a social following; Ebook 2's price asks PDF money to do a video course's job; and the "neutral educator" and "agent who wants the mandate" roles need an honest bridge, not silence.

QuestionFindingSignal
Market size~130,000–240,000 undeveloped-land sales/year in Poland; the developer-facing subset is narrower — plausibly low thousands/year, concentrated around Warsaw, Kraków, Wrocław, Tricity, Poznań.Real, narrow
CompetitionNo paid, seller-side product found anywhere. All existing free content comes from developers or land-buying intermediaries — a structural conflict of interest this brand can name openly.Clear gap
PricingEbook 1 (149 zł) sits correctly between book and course pricing. Ebook 2 (399–499 zł) is priced like a video course but delivered as a PDF — a format mismatch, not a wrong number.Needs reshaping
ChannelPlot sellers are in-market once, then gone — they don't follow land-selling accounts for years. Search intent (Google, YouTube) will outperform organic-follower building as the primary channel.Shift channel mix
English / German editionsA real German-speaking audience exists (foreign heirs of Polish land) — but it buys bilingual legal services, not ebooks. Not worth building now.Skip for now

Recommendations

  • Positioning: "the only advisor in the room paid by you, not by the buyer" — turns every free competitor's conflict of interest into this brand's core differentiator.
  • Pricing: keep Ebook 1 as-is. Rebuild Ebook 2 as a fuller system (templates, short walkthroughs) at its current price, or add a premium bundle + 1:1 consultation tier around 890–990 zł — which doubles as the agency lead funnel.
  • Funnel: free calculator/checklist → 149 zł ebook → premium bundle/consultation → agency mandate. Disclose the agency connection openly in every ebook — it reads as expertise, not a conflict, when it isn't hidden.
  • Next topics, in order: inherited/co-owned plots, the 2026 planning-law reform (plany ogólne), agricultural land sales, tax on land sale.
  • Legal housekeeping: both ebook PDFs already credit "Kanaan Land Property Investment Sp. z o.o." as copyright owner — worth re-exporting once that company is actually registered, so the rights notice is accurate.
Cheapest next test. Run the calculator below behind ~500–1,000 zł of Google/Facebook ads against "sprzedaż działki deweloperowi"-type search terms for 30 days. Email-capture and 149 zł conversion rates will answer the demand question directly — before the September price rise.
